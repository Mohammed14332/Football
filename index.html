Football is the biggest sport in the world with 4 billion estimated global following. England invented football in the year 1863! There are many competitions in football, like Champions League and World Cup.

A football match is 90 minutes long which is divided into two halves that are 45 minutes. Depending on the injuries and breaks that occur, the referee can add extra extra time in each half.
